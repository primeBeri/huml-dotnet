# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Contributors and maintainers of Huml.Net are committed to making participation in this
project a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Our Standards

We expect all participants to:

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions, code reviews, and issue threads.
- Welcome contributors of all experience levels.
- Focus feedback on code and ideas, not on individuals.
- Accept responsibility for mistakes and learn from them.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the maintainer at
[rsr@revarch.co.uk](mailto:rsr@revarch.co.uk). All reports will be reviewed promptly
and handled with discretion. The maintainer reserves the right to remove, edit, or
reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.
